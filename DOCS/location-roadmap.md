## Expansion Location Roadmap

### Real-World Inspired Packs

#### Norwegian Fjords (Arctic Pack)
- Fish: Atlantic cod, halibut, wolf fish, midnight-glow haddock  
- Twist: Aurora nights boost rare spawns; icy currents slow reeling speed.

#### Amazon Blackwater Basin (Rainforest Pack)
- Fish: Peacock bass, arapaima, redtail catfish, electric eel  
- Twist: Sudden rainstorms change visibility and lure effectiveness.

#### Great Barrier Reef (Tropical Pack)
- Fish: Giant trevally, mahi-mahi, napoleon wrasse, clown triggerfish  
- Twist: Coral “cleaning stations” where patience nets rare catches.

#### Scottish Highlands Loch (Myth & Legend Pack)
- Fish: Brown trout, pike, Atlantic salmon, elusive “Loch Lurker”  
- Twist: Fog events where sonar rods become invaluable.

#### Mongolian Steppe River (Nomad Pack)
- Fish: Taimen, lenok, golden carp, steppe grayling  
- Twist: Seasonal migrations shift hotspots around the map.

### Fantastical Signature Packs

#### Celestial Cascade
- Fish: Starstream koi, Nebula ray, Comet piranha, Lunar catfish  
- Twist: Constellation-aligned fishing windows for top-tier catches.

#### Crystal Cavern Lake
- Fish: Prism perch, Geode snapper, Quartz eel, Shatterfin  
- Twist: Light-reflecting crystals that alter bite rhythm and timing.

#### Molten Core Springs
- Fish: Lava carp, Emberscale bass, Volcano salmon, Obsidian angler  
- Twist: Heat-resistant gear needed; volcanic tremors trigger frenzies.

#### Sky Archipelago
- Fish: Nimbus guppy, Thunderfin tuna, Storm wyvern, Cloud cat  
- Twist: Gusty winds affect casting arcs; glider rods add verticality.

#### Cyber Marina (Futuristic DLC)
- Fish: Neon swordfish, Circuit shark, Byte barracuda, Hologram jelly  
- Twist: Hackable drones reveal fish data; EMP storms scramble tackle perks.

---

Each pack adds weather/time variants, gear specializations, and mini-quests so players learn new mechanics while their core skills still carry forward.
