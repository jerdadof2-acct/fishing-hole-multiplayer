# Water Effects Specifications

## Overview
This document tracks the water implementation and effects for Kitty Creek Fishing Club.

## Current Implementation

### Water Characteristics
- **Transparency:** Yes - allows visibility through water
- **Refraction:** Yes - distorted view of objects underwater
- **Reflections:** Yes - sky/environment reflections on surface
- **Fresnel Effect:** Yes - appearance changes based on viewing angle
- **Wave Animations:** Yes - gentle surface movement with ripple effects
- **Color Palette:** Cartoon-styled blue/green tints
- **Surface Movement:** Gentle, realistic water movement

### Technical Details
- Water geometry: Plane with defined bounds for lake container
- Material: Shader-based with realistic water properties
- Bounds: Defined for random spot validation (all casts must land in lake)
